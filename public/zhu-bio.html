Zhu-Tian Chen

Zhu-Tian Chen is an incoming Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the University of Minnesota-Twin Cities, currently a PostDoc Fellow at Harvard University's Visual Computing Group. His research focuses on Data Visualization, Human-Computer Interaction, and Augmented/Virtual Reality (AR/VR), with a keen interest in elevating sports experiences through AR/VR technologies. His work has been published in top conferences like IEEE VIS, ACM CHI, and TVCG, earning him multiple best paper and honorable mention awards.
